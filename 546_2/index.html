Table Saw Operation

Step 1: Safety Check

Before operating the table saw, ensure all safety guards are in place and you're wearing appropriate safety equipment.

Step 2: Blade Height Adjustment

Adjust the blade height to approximately 1/4 inch above the material you're cutting.

Step 3: Fence Alignment

Set the fence to the desired width and ensure it's parallel to the blade.

Step 4: Making the Cut

Use a push stick, keep hands clear, and feed the material steadily through the blade.
